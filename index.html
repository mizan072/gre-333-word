About This App

This application is built to help students master the 333 essential GRE vocabulary words using proven cognitive science principles.

Core Learning Method

Our system is based on two key ideas:

  • Chunking: We break the 333 words into small, 10-word lessons. This prevents you from being overwhelmed.
  • Active Recall: Instead of just re-reading (passive learning), you are immediately tested on each lesson. This act of pulling information *from* your memory is the single most effective way to build strong, long-term retention.

Features

  • Full 333-word GRE vocabulary list.
  • Progress tracking with a mastery-based system (80% to pass).
  • Spaced repetition-style "Review All Mastered Words" mode.
  • Daily random tests to check your retention.
  • Full searchable dictionary.
  • English (en-US) pronunciation for every word.
  • Dark mode support.
